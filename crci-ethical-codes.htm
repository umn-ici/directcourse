Ethical Codes

College of Recovery and Community Inclusion (CRCI)

Ethical codes or codes of conduct are part of professional practice. You need to know about any that apply to your specific licenses or certifications. Your employer may also expect you to follow a code.

Mental health professionals may find it helpful to be aware of the Psychiatric Rehabilitation Association (PRA) Code of Ethics (COE). This organization is dedicated to community-based mental health recovery. Content in CRCI follows this Code of Ethics.

The NADSP COE is helpful for any professional who works directly with people. It provides helpful support for staying person-centered and supporting self-determination. Read the full text of the NADSP COE.

You may find it helpful to refer to these or other codes when finishing reflection activities in the materials.
